Can you guess the mystery word?

You have 6 chances to guess the correct 5-letter, mystery word. Each time you submit a word, the letters will change color depending on the following criteria:

Grey: the letter does not exist in the mystery word. Tip: if you spell a word correctly but you are prompted to try a new word, the word doesn't exist in the wordbank. Try a new word!
Yellow: the letter exists but is in the wrong spot. Hint: if a letter turns yellow, it may exist more than once in the mystery word.
Green: the letter exists and is in the correct spot!

Start by clicking the keys in the keyboard below and press submit. Good Luck!
